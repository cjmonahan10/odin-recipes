Spicy Penne Pasta

This pasta dish is prefect for anyone who is looking for the perfect combination of sweet and spicy. It combines wonderful spicy italian sausage and penne pasta along with sliced pepper and a sweet tomato sauce to create a truly delectible dish.

Ingredients

Gather the items listed below to create four servings.

  • 1 Box Penne Pasta
  • 1lb Spicy Italian Sausage
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 1 Large Prego Traditional Tomato Sauce

Procedure

  1. Bring 16 quarts of water to rolling boil in a large pot.
  2. Prepare peppers by cutting into slices.
  3. Place sausages into large sauce pan and cook on medium to high heat until cooked through.
  4. Pour entire box of pasta into boiling water and cook 10 to 12 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  5. Remove cooked sausage from sauce pan and slice into small circles.
  6. Reduce heat on sauce pan to medium low and pour tomato sauce into pan.
  7. Place sausage back into sauce to keep warm.
  8. Remove pasta from heat and strain once finished cooking.
  9. Mix all ingredients together in sauce pan and serve.
