Spicy Chikin Sandwich

This delectible treat is tasty, spicy and incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

Before cooking this meal you will need to obtain the following items.

  • About 5 dollars. Preferably 10.

Procedure

Follow these steps to make a Spicy Chikin Sandwich!

  1. Start car and drive to nearest Chik-fil-a
  2. Decide wether to go inside or wait in drive through. Go inside if you are feeling energetic. Wait in the dirve through if you are feeling lazy or the weather is bad.
  3. Order a Spicy Chicken Sandwich.
  4. (Optional) If you have more than 5 dollars order fries and a drink.
  5. Wait for you name to be called and retrieve your food.
  6. Go home and enjoy.
